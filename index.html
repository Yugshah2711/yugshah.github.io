About Me

“My name is Yug Shah, originally from Ahmedabad, India. I hold a B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from PDEU, which I completed in 2022. With around two years of experience as a Manufacturing and Design Engineer, I have developed a deep understanding of industrial processes, automation, and product design.

Currently, I am pursuing my MS in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, where I am further honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. In the summer of 2024, I had the opportunity to work in South Africa as an intern, where I applied my expertise in Lean Manufacturing to support a small-scale startup’s growth. This experience allowed me to bridge theory with practical challenges, helping optimize operations and enhance productivity.

Beyond engineering, I am passionate about technology and automobiles, always eager to explore new innovations in these fields. In my free time, I enjoy playing cricket and table tennis, immersing myself in books, and watching anime.

I am actively seeking new opportunities that will allow me to contribute meaningfully while continuing to grow both personally and professionally.”
